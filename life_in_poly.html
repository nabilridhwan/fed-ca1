My current experiences in...

Singapore Polytechnic

My current experience in Singapore Polytechnic is delightful. I got to meet many different friends from many different backgrounds. The amount of freedom given to us at the tertiary level of education is a lot; We can wear any clothes we want!

School of Computing

Many people in the School of Computing have a previous background in programming, which is a relief! I get to share my passion with other people with the same passion! That makes my experience a good one.

SP MLS / SP Dikir

I am part of SP Dikir group which is a performing arts sector of SP Malay Language Society. Everyone in the CCA is very friendly, goofy and fun to be around with.
